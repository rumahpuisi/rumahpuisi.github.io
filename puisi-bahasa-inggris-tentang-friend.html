The message conveyed from the song above is to be strong and rise from sadness and not let us be overcome by feelings of ignorance just because a man cut us off.Pesan yang disampaikan dari lagu diatas adalah untuk menjadi tegar dan bangkit dari kesedihan dan tidak membiarkan kita dikalahkan oleh perasaan tidak dihargai hanya karena diputus oleh seorang lelaki.

35. Buatlah ringkasan dari novel Friend Forever by Aditi Das, dalam Bahasa Inggris


Jawaban:  

An amazing short story for beginners about friendship. The story is about a very unique teacher named Ms Rose Marie who teaches in a very unique way and knows clearly how to deal with her pupils.

The whole story is based on two questions as they were written in the story "do you really feel true friendship exists? Did you ever see a never ending bond between friends?"

She succeeded to answer her student's question through a precise story at the same time taught them a precious lesson.
