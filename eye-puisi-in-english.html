3. explain the formation of shadows in the human eye! Pls hurry (English Pls)


Jawaban:

The process of creating a shadow in the eye is this: Impulses come in waves of light entering the eye through the cornea will pass through the pupil whose pupil is fixed by the iris (light intensity regulated by the iris), then light will be refracted by the eye's lens, creating a viscous, inverted, subtracted shadow and then it will be forwarded to the rods and cones which will continue the light signals through the optic nerve to translate the impulses, The image will form on its object.

The pupil isa muscle that closes and opens or grows smaller and larger.The lens is a translucent, flexible network located right behind the iris and the pupil. The function of the lens is to help focus light and images on the retina.Choroid and conjunctiva are dark brown membranes with large blood vessels inside. Its position is between the sklera and the retina. This choroid serves to supply blood and nutrients to the retina and to all other structures in the anatomy of the eye. The conjunctivitis, on the other hand, are the transparent layers that coat the cornea and the eyelid.Vitreous humor is a jelly-like substance that fills the inside of the back of the eye.The retina and the optic are a light-sensitive network. 40. While I. Tennis,a ball. Me in the eye. (play,hit).


Jawaban:

While I Play Tennis,A Ball Hit Me In The Eye

Jawaban:

While I play tennis, a ball hits me in the eye.

Penjelasan:

Kata kerja (verb) dalam bahasa Inggris jika dalam bentuk present tense (terjadi di masa sekarang) maka bisa berubah tergantung kondisi subjeknya.

Jika subjeknya tunggal (hanya berjumlah 1), maka kata kerja tersebut akan mendapat imbuhan s / es.

Contoh:

Hit      = HitsDrink  = DrinksGo      = GoesFinish = Finishes

- A ball hits me in the eye.

- She drinks pinacolada.
