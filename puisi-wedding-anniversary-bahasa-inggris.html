First of all I would like to thank The Almighty for His never-ending blessings so we can gather here in this important event. Good morning to the honored Head Master, teachers, school administration staffs, and my friends. Today, january 1st 2018 marks the day of our school 60th anniversary. My name is Diana Alfianti, the President of Montgomery President Council and I would be delivering a speech in this very special day today. Today is a legendary day for us because this school was established 60 years ago. Let us pray that our school will be a school which produced qualified leaders who can make family, society, nation, and the country proud.

To the honorable teachers,

I am standing here to represent my friends, we would like to thank the teachers here for your hard work in nurturing us to be a good human not only for myself but family, nation, and country. My dearest teachers, what am I without you? I will not be able to know many things if you were not here, I will not be able to pursue my dreams if you were not here. In his very moment, I sincerely wishing you happiness in life, health, and I hope the Almighty will always bless your way.

As for my friends, let us use this moment to reflect upon ourselves. We had been given a chance to study here, now ask yourself, what you have done well for our school. The children in the past had no opportunity to study at school like us. They live under stupidity and never-ending pressure. We should not waste this blessing, as a student we need to have motivation in studying. This motivation will acts as a supporting factor which will help us to boost achievement, spirit, and the willingness to achieve success in the future. Actually studying hard is not enough if we did not have motivation to do better in study.

33. I am very busy tonight, so I... attend my friends wedding anniversary​


Sepertinya pertanyaanmu kurang lengkap karena tidak menyertakan pilihan jawaban untuk soal tersebut. Berikut soal lengkap beserta jawabannya.

I am very busy tonight, so I .... attend my friend's wedding anniversary.

A. can't

B. will

C. can

D. won't

Answer: A. Can’t

Penjelasan:

Can merupakan salah satu bentuk modal auxiliary yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kemampuan. Contoh, I can cook traditional dishes (Saya dapat memasak makanan tradisional). Selain itu, can juga dapat digunakan untuk meminta izin pada situasi non formal.  Contoh, Can I borrow your book? (Dapatkah aku meminjam bukumu?)

Struktur Kalimat

(+) S + can + V1 + O

(-) S + cannot + V1 + O

(?) Can + S + V1 + O + ?  

Contoh kalimat:

(+) She can go out tonight

(-) She cannot go out tonight

(?) Can she go out tonight?

Semoga membantu.
