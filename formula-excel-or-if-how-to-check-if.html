30. contoh 4 soal to check if we are understood


“Checking for understanding” adalah kalimat – kalimat yang bermaksud mengecek apakah lawan bicara sudah mengerti tujuan dan maksud kita. Contoh dialog “Checking for understanding” adalah sebagai berikut:



A: Hello, can you help me to find the way to go to X?


B: Sure. Are you taking the bus or on foot?


A: I will go by bus.


B: Ok, so from here, you can take bus number 601 in front of this building. And then you will transfer to bus 255 at M Station.


A: Yes. And after that? Where should I get off?


B: The final stop of bus 255 is N Station. You can get off there and walk about 15 minutes to X. Have you got it? Any question?


A: Yes, I got it. Thank you for your help.



Kalimat – kalimat yang dapat digunakan untuk “checking for understanding” antara lain:


1. Do you know what I mean?


2. Do you know what I'm saying?


3. Do you understand?


4. Are you following me?


5. Are you with me (so far)?


6. Have you got it?


7. Any questions?

32. Contoh dialog to check if we are understood


Contoh dialog menggunakan expression to check if we are understood

Tony: "Hey Steve, can you help me for a second?"

Steve: "What's wrong, Tony?"

Tony: "I don't know how to make the page number, would you mind teaching me?"

Steve: "Sure.  First, you just double-click on the lower part of the paper. Then go to the page number on the upper left of the window, and choose 'bottom of page'. Understand?"

Tony: "Yes, I get it. Thanks, Steve!"

Pembahasan

Terjemahan dialog menggunakan expression to check if we are understood

Tony: "Hei Steve, bisakah kamu membantuku sebentar?"

Steve: "Ada apa, Tony?"

Tony: "Saya tidak tahu bagaimana cara membuat nomor halaman, maukah kamu mengajari saya?"

Steve: "Tentu. Pertama, kamu klik dua kali pada bagian bawah kertas. Lalu pergi ke nomor halaman di kiri atas jendela, dan pilih 'bottom of page'. Paham?"

Tony: "Ya, saya mengerti. Terima kasih, Steve!"

To check if we are understood merupakan ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengetahui atau memastikan kepahaman seseorang. Ungkapan ini digunakan ketika kita memberi tahu atau menjelaskan suatu hal yang kemudian kita tanyakan kepada seseorang jika penjelasan kita bisa dimengerti atau dipahami sehingga tidak menimbulkan kesalahpahaman.

Dalam Bahasa Inggris terdapat beberapa ungkapan yang digunakan to check if we are understood, diantaranya:

Do you understand? (Apakah kamu paham?)Do you know what I mean? (Apakah kamu tahu apa maksud saya?)What do you mean? (Apa maksud kamu?)Is it clear? (Sudah jelas?)Understand? (Paham?)

39. If you want to check your CD how should you hold the CD


Jawaban:

When handling a CD (Compact Disc), it's important to hold it properly to avoid any damage. Here are some tips:

1. **Hold by the Edges**: Always hold a CD by its outer edge. Never touch the shiny, reflective surface with your fingers as it can leave smudges and fingerprints that can interfere with the disc's readability.

2. **Use the Center Hole**: You can also put your finger through the center hole of the CD for a more secure grip.

3. **Avoid Scratches**: Be careful not to scratch the disc. Even small scratches can cause skips or prevent the CD from playing entirely.

4. **Keep it Clean**: If you need to clean the CD, wipe it gently with a soft, lint-free cloth from the center of the disc straight out to the edge. Do not wipe in a circular motion as this could cause more damage.

5. **Storage**: When you're not using the CD, store it in its case to protect it from dust and scratches.

Remember, improper handling can cause permanent damage to a CD, affecting its longevity and performance.

JAWABAN :

To check a CD, you should hold it by its outer edge and avoid touching the shiny, reflective surface to prevent smudges or scratches.
